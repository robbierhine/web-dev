Ann-Sofie Back Fall/Winter 2004

Untitled Collection


Photo:Chris Moore
Styling:Ann-Sofie Back,Mattias Karlsson, and Robert Rydberg
Hair:Rudy Lewis
Make-Up:Linda Öhrström
Location:Balden-Powell House, London

"This year I had my first design assistant, Anna-Sara Davik. Since we were two, the design output was enormous."

"I don't even know what the collection is about, it just felt like an outpouring of ideas that became the sum."

"I have made up explanations afterwards but whilst working there was no special direction."
"Ann-Sofie Back's 'Blouse Bag' eschews flashy, recognisable styling and aims to literally blend into its wearer's outfit." -Penny Martin forShowStudio

"Though marketed by the designer to 'the women that aspire toward glamour but fall short of achieving it', the Blouse Bag underlines the alchemic power of Ann-Sofie Back and her singular capacity for grace." -Penny Martin forShowStudio
