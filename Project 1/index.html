During this time, the UK tabloid press was at its worst. Women were hounded, shamed and ridiculed on a daily basis. I don't think you can imagine the level of double standards and misogynism for women that was accepted and normalised, packaged and then sold back to women. This phenomenon was bothering me for years but I didn't know how to be inspired by it. After some years I stopped thinking that I had to offer a solution, and I think this is one of my best collec-tions. (But now, today, I'm really bored with the look.)
